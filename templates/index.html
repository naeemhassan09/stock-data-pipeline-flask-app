Optimized Portfolio Prediction

In this approach, we use historical training data (January 2022 – December 2024) to compute each asset’s average daily return. Assets with higher historical performance receive a higher allocation. The optimized weights are computed proportionally to each asset's average daily return, which may better capture differences in performance between stocks and benchmarks.
